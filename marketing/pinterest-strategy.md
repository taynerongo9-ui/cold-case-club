## Pin Optimization Tips

- **Keywords in title and first 50 characters** of description (Pinterest SEO is keyword-driven)
- **2:3 aspect ratio** images (1000x1500px) for maximum visibility
- **Text overlay** on images — Pinterest is visual-first, text helps stop the scroll
- **Schedule pins** for 8-11 PM and weekends (peak Pinterest usage)
- **5-10 pins per day** to new + existing boards
- **Rich pins enabled** via website claim
- **Alt text** on all pin images with keywords
