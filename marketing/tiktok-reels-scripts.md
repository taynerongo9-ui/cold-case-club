# Cold Case Club — TikTok / Instagram Reels Script Templates

> **Purpose:** 5 short-form video templates for organic social content.
> **Target:** Women 25-55, true crime fans, mystery lovers, gift shoppers.
> **Tone:** Intriguing, slightly eerie, premium. Never cheesy.
> **Music:** Dark ambient, lo-fi noir, suspenseful piano.

---

## Script 1: "The Unboxing" (15-30 seconds)

**Hook (0-3s):** Close-up of hands holding a sealed cream envelope with a wax stamp. Text overlay: "Someone got away with murder."

**Body (3-20s):**
- Slow open of the envelope, pulling out aged paper documents
- Fan out the evidence pieces on a dark wooden table
- Close-up of a witness statement with highlighted text
- Close-up of a newspaper clipping headline
- Close-up of a coded message
- Hands pinning a photo to a corkboard with red string

**CTA (20-30s):** Text overlay: "12 evidence packets. 6 months. 1 cold case to solve." + "Link in bio"

**Caption:** "This isn't a subscription box. It's a crime scene in your mailbox. #coldcaseclub #truecrime #mysterysubscription #unboxing"

**Notes:** Film in warm lamp light on a dark surface. No face needed — just hands and evidence. Use ASMR-style audio (paper rustling, envelope tearing).

## Script 2: "The Reaction" (15-30 seconds)

**Hook (0-3s):** Person reading a document, eyes go wide. Text overlay: "When the evidence doesn't match the alibi..."

**Body (3-20s):**
- Person flipping between two documents, pointing at contradictions
- Close-up of a timeline with red circles
- Person pulling out a magnifying glass (dramatic zoom)
- Quick cut to their investigation board — photos, string, notes everywhere
- Whispered voiceover: "Wait... the coat. The coat on the bridge wasn't hers."

**CTA (20-30s):** Text overlay: "coldcaseclub.au — Real evidence. Real mystery. Real mail." + "Starting at $12.50/mo"

**Caption:** "When you catch the detail that changes EVERYTHING 🔍 Month 3 hit different. #truecrime #coldcase #detective #fyp"

**Notes:** This works best with a real subscriber. Can be staged initially. The "aha moment" is the money shot.

## Script 3: "What's Inside Each Packet" (30-60 seconds)

**Hook (0-3s):** Dark desk, dramatic lighting. Text overlay: "What $12.50/month gets you"

**Body (3-45s):**
- Reveal each evidence type one by one, placing them on the desk:
  1. "Detective's notes" — handwritten page slides in
  2. "Witness statements" — typed interview transcript
  3. "Newspaper clippings" — vintage newspaper with headline visible
  4. "Crime scene photos" — black and white photograph
  5. "Coded messages" — paper with cipher text, magnifying glass placed over it
  6. "Surprise artifacts" — matchbook, torn ticket, old key placed dramatically
- Each item gets a 3-5 second lingering shot
- Voiceover: "Every two weeks, a new packet arrives. Real paper. Real mystery. Six months to figure out who did it."

**CTA (45-60s):** Full evidence spread visible. Text overlay: "The case is open. Link in bio." + Cold Case Club logo

**Caption:** "This is not a game. This is an investigation. 📎 #mysterybox #truecrime #subscription #evidence #coldcaseclub"

**Notes:** This is the "explainer" video. Film slow and cinematic. No rush. Let each item breathe.

## Script 4: "Cracking the Code" (15-30 seconds)

**Hook (0-3s):** Close-up of the coded message. Text overlay: "They said no one could crack it."

**Body (3-20s):**
- Hands writing letters underneath the coded text
- Crossing out attempts, circling correct letters
- Dramatic pause — then the decoded message appears:
  "RICHARD KNOWS I FOUND THE ACCOUNTS"
- Camera slowly pulls back to show investigation board with Richard's photo circled in red
- Dramatic music hit

**CTA (20-30s):** Text overlay: "Can you solve it before the final packet? coldcaseclub.au"

**Caption:** "When the code cracked and I literally gasped 😱 This changes EVERYTHING about who I suspected. #cipher #codebreaking #truecrime #coldcaseclub"

**Notes:** The cipher solve is inherently satisfying content. Show the process, not just the answer. The dramatic reveal of the decoded message is the hook.

---

## Script 5: "Gift Reaction" (15-30 seconds)

**Hook (0-3s):** Gift box being handed to someone. Text overlay: "I got my mom a mystery subscription for Mother's Day..."

**Body (3-20s):**
- Mom opening the box, pulling out the first envelope
- Her face going from confusion to excitement
- Reading the first document, eyes scanning
- Cut to: her home office wall, 3 months later — FULL investigation board
- Her laughing/pointing at suspects, wine glass in hand
- Close-up of her texting: "I KNOW WHO DID IT"

**CTA (20-30s):** Text overlay: "6 months later she still hasn't shut up about it. Best $75 I ever spent." + "coldcaseclub.au/gift"

**Caption:** "She went from 'what is this' to full detective mode in 48 hours 😂 Best gift I've ever given. #mothersday #giftidea #truecrime #coldcaseclub"

**Notes:** Gift reactions perform EXTREMELY well. Film the actual reaction if possible. The "3 months later" investigation board shot is optional but powerful.

## Production Tips

1. **Lighting:** Single warm desk lamp, darkness everywhere else. The noir look sells itself.
2. **Sound:** Paper rustling ASMR + dark ambient music. Never use trending audio for brand videos.
3. **Camera:** iPhone is fine. Overhead (flat lay) for evidence spreads, eye-level for reactions.
4. **Editing:** Slow, deliberate cuts. This isn't a dance video. Let the mystery breathe.
5. **Text:** Use a monospace/typewriter font for text overlays. Stay on brand.
6. **Frequency:** 3-4 posts/week minimum for algorithm favor. Batch film 2 weeks of content.
