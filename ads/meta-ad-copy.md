## AD CREATIVE SPECS

| Placement | Dimensions | Max File Size | Video Length |
|-----------|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Feed (FB/IG) | 1080×1080 or 1080×1350 | 30 MB | 15-60s |
| Stories/Reels | 1080×1920 (9:16) | 30 MB | 10-30s |
| Carousel | 1080×1080 per card | 30 MB | N/A |

### Creative Notes
- Dark backgrounds (match site aesthetic)
- Show REAL evidence packets — not mockups. Print samples and photograph them.
- Hands opening envelopes = highest-performing creative in subscription ads
- Include price in ad copy (qualifies clicks, lowers wasted spend)
- Always include the guarantee ("30-day money-back")
- Use urgency ONLY when real (Mother's Day cutoff, limited first run)
