## BIDDING STRATEGY RECOMMENDATIONS

### Phase 1: Learning (Weeks 1-4)
- **All campaigns:** Maximise Clicks with daily budget caps
- **Brand Search:** Target Impression Share (95%+ top of page)
- **Daily budget:** $20-30 per campaign, $50 for Brand Search
- **Goal:** Collect 30+ conversions per campaign for Smart Bidding

### Phase 2: Optimisation (Weeks 5-8)
- Switch to **Maximise Conversions** once each campaign hits 30 conversions
- Set target CPA based on actual data:
  - Brand Search: tCPA $15-25
  - Category Search: tCPA $40-50
  - Competitor: tCPA $45-55
  - Gift Intent: tCPA $40-50
  - Problem/Solution: tCPA $50-60
  - Long-tail: tCPA $35-45

### Phase 3: Scale (Weeks 9+)
- Shift budget to lowest-CPA campaigns
- Test **Target ROAS** bidding if average order value data is strong
- Increase Brand Search budget to defend against competitors
- Scale Gift Intent 3 weeks before Mother's Day, Father's Day, Christmas
