About Us

The Story Behind Cold Case Club

Why We Started Cold Case Club

We're true crime obsessives who wanted something deeper than a podcast. Something you could hold in your hands. Something that made you feel like you were actually working the case.

So we built it.

Cold Case Club delivers real evidence packets to your mailbox — detective notes, witness statements, coded messages, newspaper clippings — and gives you 6 months to crack a cold case. No screens. No apps. Just paper, clues, and your instincts.

We're based in Australia, and we ship nationwide. Every case is written, designed, and assembled by our team. There's no algorithm deciding what you get next — just a carefully constructed investigation that unfolds piece by piece.

What We Believe

Stories First

Every case is a complete narrative. Not a puzzle for puzzle's sake, but a story with real characters, real motives, and real stakes.

Paper Over Pixels

Physical evidence creates a different kind of immersion. The texture of a handwritten note. The weight of a sealed envelope. Things a screen can't replicate.

Affordable Mystery

Great experiences shouldn't break the bank. Starting at $14.99/mo, we're the most affordable mystery subscription available.

No Spoilers, Ever

Our cases are designed so nobody — not even search engines — can ruin your investigation.

How It Works

Pick a plan and subscribe. Every two weeks, a new evidence packet arrives at your door — 12 packets total over 6 months. Each one adds new leads, new suspects, and new contradictions to your case file.

By the end, you'll have everything you need to build a theory. Submit it. Then we send the reveal — the full truth behind the case. Some investigators get it right. Most are surprised.
